HERE'S A LITTLE BIT ABOUT US

Who are we?

We are college students currently attending the University of Kentucky. We want to change the way in which our peers and society view data collection. It's time that our generation, Generation Z, pays more attention to where our data is going and why it is being collected.

What is our objective?

Our objective is to make a difference in our community in a way that is both important and sustainable. Our major initiative is to inform our peers, college students, about the potential dangers of companies capturing, owning, and selling our data. Our goal is to provide methods for preventing the unknown and unwelcome relinquishing of this data. A secondary initiative, if given the opportunity, is to "loudly" support legislation that aims to protect citizens from the dangers of the internet.
